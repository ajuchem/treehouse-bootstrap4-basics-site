About Full Stack Cat Conf

The beautiful city of Denver, Colorado will be the host city for Full Stack Cat Conf!

Explore the future of Cats with a lineup of industry professionals. Discover new techniques to advance your career as a cat developer.

Expert Speakers

Our expert speaker lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before grabbing your tickets!

Want to meet the international Cat community and share skills with some of the world's top experts, hackers, and makers? Be the first to know what to expect for the future of Cats.

Cat Conf is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to another intensive day of learning and sharing.

What You'll Learn

MoncatDB: NoSQL database Cangular: CatScript framework Catxpress: Framework for Node Nodecat.js: CatScript environment ESCAT2015: Latest version of CatScript Catbel: CatScript compiler

Speakers

Angie McCat

Angie is a cat developer and teacher who is passionate about building scalable, data driven cat apps, specially ones that address old problems with new tech!

Nodecatdamus

"NodeCat" is a software engineer and philosopher trying to leave the world better than he found it. He codes for non-profits and large-scale cat apps.

Geo "Lo" Cation

Geo is a CatScript developer working on large-scale cat applications. He's also a teacher who strives to support cats in removing all barriers to learning code.

Ecma Scriptnstuff

Ecma found her passion for cats and programming over 15 years ago. She is excited to introduce cats to the wonderful world of CatScript.

Jay Query

Jay is a developer, author of CSS: The Cat Manual, CatScript & jQuery: The Cat Manual, and cat development teacher.

Json Babel

All of his professional life, Json has worked with cats online; he is a polyglot cat programmer and likes using the right tools for the job.
